Example Tasks for CloudFactory

Please try one or more of these example tasks to get an idea of work done. You need to do it as a CloudFactory Cloud Worker. This should enable you to get a general idea of how much you can earn through such tasks.
Extract information from given grave stone.
INSTRUCTIONS

Instructions

(If this is your first time doing this type of work, you may want to take a look at sample work 1, sample work 2).

  • If the image is completely not readable or blurry, check the 'image is not readable' and SUBMIT the hit.
  • Carefully identify the number of people in the grave stone and check the appropriate number radio button.
  • If there are more than two people in the gravestone image, click the 'More than Two' radio button then enter the number in the text box and press 'Ok'. This will generate the form accordingly.
  • Extract all information from the given grave stone image and fill them into the appropriate field of the form.
  • If the field does not exists in the grave stone image then leave the field blank. For example, if the image has no eulogy then leave the field blank in the form, do not add your own eulogy.
  • If an item in the grave stone image is partially readable, enter which is as much as readable data. A best guess data is better than the empty field.
If the gravestone carries more than one dead person name, select the right number:
